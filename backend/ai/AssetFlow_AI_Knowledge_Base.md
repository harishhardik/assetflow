# AssetFlow AI Knowledge Base (15 Records)

## Record 1

Question: How do I register a new asset? Answer: Go to Asset Registry,
click "Register Asset", fill in the asset details such as name,
category, serial number, location, and condition, then save. The asset
status is set to Available.

## Record 2

Question: How do I allocate an asset? Answer: Open the asset details,
click Allocate, select the employee or department, choose the expected
return date, and confirm. The asset status changes to Allocated.

## Record 3

Question: What happens if an asset is already allocated? Answer:
AssetFlow prevents double allocation. The system displays the current
holder and suggests creating a transfer request instead.

## Record 4

Question: How do I return an asset? Answer: Open the active allocation,
click Return Asset, enter the condition notes, and submit. The asset
status changes back to Available.

## Record 5

Question: Who can approve maintenance requests? Answer: Only the Asset
Manager can approve or reject maintenance requests. After approval, the
asset status changes to Under Maintenance.

## Record 6

Question: What is the asset lifecycle? Answer: Available → Allocated →
Returned → Available or Under Maintenance → Available. Retired or Lost
assets are removed from active usage.

## Record 7

Question: How do I book a shared resource? Answer: Open the Bookings
page, choose the resource, select the date and time, and submit. The
system checks for scheduling conflicts before confirming.

## Record 8

Question: Why was my booking rejected? Answer: The requested time
overlaps with an existing booking. Choose another available time slot.

## Record 9

Question: What are overdue returns? Answer: Assets not returned before
the expected return date are automatically marked as overdue and appear
on the dashboard.

## Record 10

Question: What roles are available in AssetFlow? Answer: Admin, Asset
Manager, Department Head, and Employee. Each role has different
permissions.

## Record 11

Question: What is an audit cycle? Answer: An audit cycle verifies asset
availability and condition. Auditors mark assets as Verified, Missing,
or Damaged, and discrepancy reports are generated.

## Record 12

Question: How do notifications work? Answer: Users receive notifications
for asset allocation, maintenance approval, overdue returns, transfers,
and booking updates.

## Record 13

Question: Can I transfer an allocated asset? Answer: Yes. Submit a
transfer request. After approval by the Asset Manager, the asset is
reassigned to the new employee or department.

## Record 14

Question: What information is shown on the dashboard? Answer: The
dashboard displays available assets, allocated assets, maintenance
requests, active bookings, overdue returns, pending transfers, and quick
actions.

## Record 15

Question: What questions can AssetFlow AI answer? Answer: AssetFlow AI
can answer questions about assets, allocations, bookings, maintenance,
audits, dashboard features, reports, notifications, roles, and system
workflows. It only responds to AssetFlow-related queries.
